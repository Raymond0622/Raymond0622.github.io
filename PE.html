Project Euler

Project Euler are set of computational/mathematical problems that are honestly pretty fun and exciting to solve. Most problems do require some deep levels of number theory and combinatorics. Regardless, they are good way of learning these concepts along with efficient algorthims to compute efficiently. The #1 rule is to calculate the answer in less than a minute of time!!
