I must be honest. Growing up playing the cello was nice, having my mom, who majored in both Piano and Cello definitely helped me, both financially and musically.

It was rough playing both instruments. Hence, I dropped the piano and focused on Piano. But, now I regret it. I started picking up the piano again... and here I am

For the past 4 years in college, I have been playing the cello every. I throughly enjoyed it. To be frank with you all, we are running out of repertoire... Nonetheless, we still meet up and enjoy the music we sightread and practice!
